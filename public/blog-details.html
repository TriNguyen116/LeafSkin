VEGAN COSMETICS

The LeafSkin Shop certified 100% vegan for all beauty products from the Vegan Association

  • by Ema Timahe
  • Seb 17, 2019
  • 39 Comments

On January 3, 2024, The Body Shop was recognized by The Vegan Society as the first global brand to achieve 100% vegan certification in all beauty products.

That is the result of the efforts that The Body Shop has made during 3 decades of operation with core values ​​towards the environment, community and nature. The brand has continuously strived to combat animal testing and is committed to 100% vegan products.

In 2021, The Body Shop, the first cosmetics retailer, achieved the ambitious goal of having 60% of products certified Vegan.

We did it! Every product formulation from The Body Shop has been certified by the Vegan Society. We understand that skin health is important to millions of people worldwide and we have worked tirelessly to achieve this important milestone.

For three decades, since launching the campaign against animal testing in 1989, the brand has never rested because there are still many poor animals being tortured and harmed to serve beauty. The Body Shop believes that no animal, large or small, should suffer or be tested in the creation of its products. As the fight for a world ban on animal testing continues, certifying 100% vegan products is a major milestone in the fight against animal testing.
